Edmondo Di Giuseppe, PhD
Climate Data Scientist
Institute for Biometeorology CNR-IBIMET
National Research Council
My work bridges statistical methodology and software tools to analyze climate phenomena and their impacts.

I use statistical tools such as functional clustering, Bayesian inference, and Extreme Value Distributions to analyze drought and heat waves. I use to combine R and CDO operators to perform analyses. I am also interested in the study of the effects of climate variability on cereals production and prices.
